Enterprise AI Search · Work AI Platforms · Knowledge Management

Glean operates at the intersection of three converging markets: enterprise search, knowledge management, and agentic Work AI platforms. Business Model

Revenue Type: Annual recurring SaaS subscription (per-user, per-month). Evolving to hybrid: base per-user license + consumption credits (Enterprise Flex model) for advanced agentic AI usage.

Pricing: ~$50+/user/month; minimum enterprise contract $60K–$100K/year. Large deployments $200K–$480K fully loaded. Custom pricing for $1M+ contracts (tripled in 2025).

Consumption Risk: Enterprise Flex model introduces variable costs. As agentic workloads scale, inference costs rise — creating CFO scrutiny at renewal. This is an emerging commercial risk that the AIOM function must address proactively with ROI documentation.

Strategic Investors (Beyond Financial Capital)

Capital One Ventures, Citi, Databricks Ventures, and Workday Ventures are investors who are also customers or ecosystem partners — a deliberate enterprise distribution strategy. Wellington Management (Series F lead) signals institutional-grade validation and pre-IPO capital readiness.
